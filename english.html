Our love story

It is said that when one finds the man or a woman of their dreams, you look at life in perspective and everything seems to fit, just like the pieces of a puzzle solved by some kind of predestination. You look at life as a succession of pinpoints on a roadmap, just like the invisible red string that connects people that are destined to meet whether real and unbreakable, as if time, place and circumstance have conspired to bring us to this exact point. This is how our story begins with a "like" from many kilometers away, with different cultures and languages. Nights of long conversations, full of emotions, uncertainty until we met for the first time, where we had no idea what God had prepared for us, that a like would be a forever. From that moment, we’ve been building our love story, we keep on ounting down the days to meet again. Distance has not been an obstacle in our relationship, it has made it stronger, it has taught us trust, the importance of a hug, to value every second together and that what is meant to be will be. We continue to write our always and forever under God's will and trusting "The best is yet to come".
